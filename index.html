From the Director

A Welcome Message

"

Welcome to High School Musical Jr. — a show about breaking boundaries, finding your voice, and discovering that you're more than just one thing.


These incredible students have poured their hearts into every rehearsal, every song, and every step. Tonight, they share that passion with you. We hope this performance reminds you — and them — that when we work together, when we lift each other up, we truly are all in this together.


Thank you for being here to celebrate them. Sit back, enjoy the show, and let yourself be swept away by the magic of live theatre.

Ms. Faride Jamid
Director · Ammons Drama
